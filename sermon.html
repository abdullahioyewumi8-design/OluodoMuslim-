How to get closer to Allah

Closeness to Allah is achieved through sincere worship, following the Qur’an and Sunnah, constant remembrance (dhikr), and striving for good character. By praying regularly, seeking knowledge, giving charity, and repenting sincerely, a believer strengthens their bond with Allah and finds peace in their heart.

Importance of Hajj in Islam

Hajj is one of the five pillars of Islam and a sacred act of worship that unites Muslims from all over the world. It symbolizes submission to Allah, reminds believers of equality and brotherhood, and serves as a journey of spiritual purification and renewal. For those who are able, performing Hajj is both an obligation and a profound opportunity to strengthen faith.

Importance of “Pillar” of Islam

The Five Pillars of Islam are the core of a Muslim’s faith. They strengthen belief in Allah, guide daily worship, promote kindness and charity, teach self-discipline through fasting, and unite Muslims worldwide through prayer and pilgrimage. Together, they build a strong foundation for spiritual growth and community life.
